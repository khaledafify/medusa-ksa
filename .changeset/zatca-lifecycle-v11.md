Make B2C Simplified invoices tax-correct for discounts, shipping,
tax-inclusive pricing, and multiple VAT rates. Add reported ZATCA credit notes
and debit notes for refunds, returns, cancellations, and post-issuance order
edits, with fail-closed reconciliation and admin remediation for rejected or
failed documents. Derive inclusive and exclusive tax bases from Medusa's final
computed totals, and keep local reconciliation failures outside the ICV/PIH
chain so numerically wrong documents are never reported.
